Who We Are

India's First Digital Salon

Studieo7 Family Salon and Bridal Studio is an award-winning premium salon chain based in South India, recognized as India's First-Ever Digital Salon with groundbreaking metaverse integration.

With 26 years of makeup expertise since 2000 and 4+ years of excellence in Tiruchirappalli, we've completed hundreds of bridal transformations and served thousands of satisfied families at our PLA Plaza, Cantonment location.

Our luxurious, hygienic environment features certified stylists who exclusively use international premium brands — MAC, NARS, Huda Beauty, L'Oréal, and Dior — to deliver flawless, personalized results for every client.

From everyday haircuts to dream bridal looks, our talented team personalizes every service with passion, precision, and the latest trends from the world of beauty.

Our Journey

26 Years of Excellence

2000 — The Beginning

Started our journey in makeup artistry, building expertise through years of dedicated practice and training.

2010 — Professional Growth

Expanded into professional services, training with international brands and mastering HD & Airbrush techniques.

2020 — Studieo7 Chain Founded

Launched the Studieo7 brand as India's first digital salon chain with metaverse integration and premium positioning.

2022 — Trichy Launch

Opened our flagship Trichy outlet at PLA Plaza, Cantonment — bringing premium beauty services to the city.

2026 — 1,164+ Reviews & Counting

Trichy's highest-rated salon with 4.9-star Google rating, award-winning recognition, and an expanding franchise network.
